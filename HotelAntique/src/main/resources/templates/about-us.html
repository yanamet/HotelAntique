Hotel Antique is a charming boutique hotel located in the heart of the historic Old Town of Plovdiv, Bulgaria. The hotel is housed in a beautifully restored 19th- century building that retains much of its original architecture and features.

The hotel's interior is tastefully decorated with antique furnishings and art pieces, creating an elegant and unique atmosphere.

Hotel Antique offers a range of room options to suit different needs and preferences, from cozy single rooms to luxurious suites with balconies overlooking the Old Town.
